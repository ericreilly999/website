About the show 01 / 03

Prompted gives you more information, not more opinions.

Hard data, real-world examples, and expert perspectives on the topics shaping technology — so you can draw your own conclusions.

Every episode starts with a prompt and takes shape through an iterative back-and-forth with an LLM — I push on the framing, challenge weak claims, and steer the narrative until the script earns its runtime. Sources are pulled, cross-checked, and verified before anything ships. OpenAI's text-to-speech handles narration. The goal is short, sourced, useful — usually under 20 minutes, always with the receipts.
